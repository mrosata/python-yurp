Guru

Welcome to Guru, a minimalistic approach to blogging. How minimal? Take your blog, and remove the entire frontend. That is the goal here, to publish nothing on location, just provide a means to creating entries and responding to API calls from whitelisted domains. You may own and operate an application of which makes requests to your personal Guru API, but the idea behind Guru is to allow publishing on many platforms from one safe and centralized location. Since I'm still in the process of creating Guru, take caution, and report any major errors directly to me.
