Guru

Welcome to Guru, a minimalistic approach to blogging. How minimal? Take your blog, and remove the entire frontend. That is the goal here, to publish nothing on location, just provide a means to creating entries and responding to API calls from whitelisted domains. You may own and operate an application of which makes requests to your personal Guru API, but the idea behind Guru is to allow publishing on many platforms. Law of Seperation, many people try to write on their blog and then publish the content across their own site as well as social platforms such as Twitter, or Facebook, and that's fine... but why tie your data to something you may not use in 12 months. Guru is where you write, your data is stored locally or to the cloud, and then published on any platform. The graphical interface is purely to support authors and developers in building a RESTFul web API using JSON to answer requests for content. This is beta, many features are either not implemented or not yet dreamed up. Flasq2json is written in Python using Flask as a WSGI and SQLAlchemy as an SQLite ORM to manage data. The "website" is designed using Twitter Bootstrap
